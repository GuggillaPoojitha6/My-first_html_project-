HIT: The 3rd Case

HIT: The Third Case (2025) is a dark, intense crime thriller starring Nani as Arjun Sarkaar, a ruthless cop chasing a string of brutal murders. The film impresses with its gritty visuals, strong performances, and action-packed scenes, but the storyline loses focus in the second half, leaning more on violence than smart investigation. Srinidhi Shetty adds emotional depth, while Prateik Babbar makes a menacing villain. Though not as tight or mysterious as the earlier HIT films, it still delivers a stylish, high-energy ride for fans of crime-action dramas.
